No Secret to Success

Sep 21, 2023

Who doesn’t know the secret to success?

Working towards anything gets you closer to success. You know it, I know it, I think all know it?

And perhaps we all even know what we need to work on to reach there.. Fitness? Eat healthy and exercise Dream Job? Prove your skills Leave a habit? Stay distracted from it Make friends? Be between people

Why do we not do it then? Are we just too weak and lazy?
