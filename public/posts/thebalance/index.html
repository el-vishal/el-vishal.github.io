All about the balance

Aug 16, 2023

It’s all about the balance - don’t you think so?

Think about it.. Extremes have always lead to worst experiences..

Too stubborn? no! Too modest? no!

Too arrogant? hell no! Too helpful? ah that’s too much!

Workholic? eww! Yolo? grr!

Greedy? jail! Generous? cottage!

Reminds me of Aristotle’s Golden Mean

all virtues lie at a mean between extremes of deficiency and excess. Golden mean is an approach to ethics that emphasizes finding the appropriate medium, or middle ground, between extremes

But then again, can you achieve the top podium without any sacrifice?

Where do you draw the line? Where do you find yourself finally content?
Perhaps it’s the curse of attempting to be the best/middle in all.. that you end up being the jack of all trades, but master of none.
